YardQuest

You are a kid playing in your treehouse, and your mom calls to you to come in for dinner. To do so, you must navigate through the backyard, which comes to life through your vivid imagination.

01

THE STORY

Game Objective

Player: First person, "Homey"
Location: Backyard Treehouse
Goal: Navigate through three zones of the backyard to get to the house for dinner
Circumstances: Player switches temporarily from their imaginative state to reality throughout the game

The Sandbox

Your first challenges will lie in a scorching, expansive desert.

The Swimming Pool

Watch out. Those oceanic creatures can swim better than you.

The Herb Garden

Your last challenge. Can you make it through the dense forest?
